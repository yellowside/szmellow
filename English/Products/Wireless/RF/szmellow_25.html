Technical Specification

 

Modulation mode:  GFSK

RF Line-of-sight Rang: 2000m(BER=10-5@9600bps),3000m(BER=10-5@1200bps)

Working Frequency:  433~915Mhz (customized)

Interface:TTL/RS232/RS485

RF power:≤500mW

Receiving sensitivity: -120-115dBm  

Reveive current: ≤32mA

Transmitting current: ≤500mA
